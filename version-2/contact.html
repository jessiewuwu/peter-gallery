Peter Cochrane

Peter Cochrane is an artist, author, and editor. He produced the photography publication HOLLOWAY featuring artists who were educated in state-funded institutions. His work has appeared in BOMB magazine, Headmaster magazine, The San Francisco Chronicle, and more. He has created books that have traveled to the Vancouver Art/Book Fair, the Los Angeles Art Book Fair, multiple Bay Area book fairs, and the Room&Book fair at the ICA in London. He has been featured in numerous exhibitions in the Bay Area including at the de Young museum.
